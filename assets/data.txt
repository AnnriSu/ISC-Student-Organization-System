Iskonnovators Student Community 

I. Brief history
• Founded on September 13, 2021 as Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) PUP-STB by Ms. Rose Evangelyn Bellen.
• Created to bridge the gap between theory and practice, empowering PUP-STC students to build real technological solutions aligned with community needs and the UN Sustainable Development Goals
• Awarded “Most Dynamic Non-Academic Organization” of PUP-STC
• Grew through workshops, bootcamps, leadership trainings, and hackathons—strengthening partnerships and producing competitive student developers
• In 2024, the organization expanded its scope beyond Google developer tools and rebranded as the ISKOnnovators Student Community (ISC)
• Today, ISC serves as a hub for student innovators, promoting technology, research, and interdisciplinary project creation for community impact

II. vision
• This organization envisions itself as a vibrant community of student innovators, creative thinkers,
and problem solvers who are passionate about the interdisciplinary creation, learning, and
transfer of technology towards the betterment of society.

III. Mission
• Upskilling its members through hands-on, project-based learning, peer mentoring, and advanced training sessions.
• Promoting emerging technologies, startups, and an innovation mindset through masterclasses, workshops, and publications.
• Nurturing members to create meaningful technological solutions for the community and represent PUP STC with excellence in competitions.
• Providing coaching and organizational support for hackathons, research initiatives, and innovation challenges.
• Exposing members to opportunities that broaden their technological experience and foster professional growth
